About Us

This website was created for the upliftment and engagement of univeristy students. It was created by individuals who saw the need for a free platform where students could access an exercising regime. Finance is always a concern for many students but it should not be adherence to not getting in shape or staying healthy. This forum provides just that. Free exercises from professionals to help stay fit throughtout their tenure.

The services offered by this website are entirely free as we are a subsidary of a non-profit organization. Therefore, to continue doing what we do and bringing this great service to you on our platform, we ask for your monitary support to help keep our doors open. These donations will go torwards our employees and other contributors of this site. Thank you for thinking of us and continue to enjoy our services.

*Disclaimer: Before starting any exercises remember to warm up to avoid risk of injuries. Also if you have any underlying medical conditons consult your doctor before starting these sessions. This site is purely for the benefit of students or any other personels. These set are given by trained and professional trainers. If you do make donations to the site, your card information would not be stored nor remembered by the site. Therefore your financial identity is safe.
